Zinger Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

    Chicken fillets
    Flour
    Eggs
    Breadcrumbs
    Mayonnaise
    Lettuce
    Tomato slices
    Spices (salt, pepper, paprika, etc.)

Instructions:

    Marinate chicken fillets with spices.
    Coat fillets with flour, dip in beaten eggs, and coat with breadcrumbs.
    Fry until golden brown and crispy.
    Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato slices, and mayonnaise.
    Place the fried chicken fillet in the burger.
    Serve and enjoy your delicious Zinger Burger!

Enjoy your homemade Zinger Burger!

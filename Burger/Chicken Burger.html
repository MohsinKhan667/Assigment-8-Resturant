Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

    Burger buns,
    Ground chicken,
    Lettuce, Tomato slices,
    Onion slices,Cheese slices,
    Mayonnaise,Ketchup,Mustard, Salt and pepper to taste,

Instructions:

    Season ground chicken with salt and pepper. Form chicken patties and grill or cook in a pan until fully cooked. Toast burger buns until golden brown.
    Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and cheese. Place the cooked chicken patty on the assembled burger. Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard on the bun as desired. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.

Enjoy your homemade Chicken Burger!

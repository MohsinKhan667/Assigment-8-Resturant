Vegetable Pulao Recipe

Ingredients:

    1 cup basmati rice
    1 large onion, thinly sliced 2 tomatoes, chopped
    1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    2-3 green chilies, chopped 1 inch ginger, grated
    3-4 garlic cloves, minced 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    1 teaspoon garam masala Salt to taste
    2 cups water Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
    2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.), chopped

Instructions:

    Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain. In a large pan, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add grated ginger and minced garlic. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes are soft. Add mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Now, add the drained basmati rice and sauté for 2 minutes, ensuring the rice is well-coated with the spices. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Vegetable Pulao is ready to be served. Enjoy it hot with raita or a side salad!

Enjoy your delicious Vegetable Pulao!

Kashmiri Pulao Recipe

Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice, 1/2 cup mixed dry fruits (cashews, almonds, raisins), 1/2 cup fresh or frozen green peas, 1 large onion, thinly sliced, 1 medium-sized carrot, finely chopped, 1/4 cup ghee or clarified butter, 2 cups water, 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds, 4-5 green cardamom pods, 4-5 cloves, 1-inch cinnamon stick, 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds, Salt to taste, Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish),

Instructions:

Wash the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain the water. In a pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds, green cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and fennel seeds. Sauté for a minute until the spices release their aroma. Add sliced onions and cook until they become golden brown. Add chopped carrots, green peas, and mixed dry fruits. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender. Add the soaked and drained basmati rice. Stir gently to coat the rice with the spices and vegetables. Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt. Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve the fragrant Kashmiri Pulao hot with yogurt or raita.

Enjoy your delicious Kashmiri Pulao!

Beef Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

    2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
    500g beef, cut into pieces
    1 cup yogurt

Marinade:

In a bowl, mix together yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Add the beef pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight.

Instructions:

    Cook the rice until it is 70-80% cooked. Drain and set aside.
    Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add sliced onions and cook until golden brown and crispy. Remove half for garnish.

Layering the Biryani:

In a heavy-bottomed pot or a biryani pot, layer half of the partially cooked rice. Top it with the cooked beef mixture. Add the remaining rice on top. Drizzle saffron-infused milk over the rice. Sprinkle fried onions and chopped coriander/mint leaves.

Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking):

Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal with aluminum foil. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the rice to fully cook.

Serve:

Gently fluff the biryani with a fork. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.

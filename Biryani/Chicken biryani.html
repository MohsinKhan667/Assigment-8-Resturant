Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

    2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
    4 cups water

Instructions:

    In a bowl, mix together yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.

    Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight.

Serve:

Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken biryani!

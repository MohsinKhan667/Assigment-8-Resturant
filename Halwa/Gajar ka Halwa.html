Gajar Ka Halwa Recipe

Ingredients:

1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter) 1/4 cup raisins
1 cup sugar 1 kg carrots, grated 1 liter full-fat milk

Instructions:

Heat ghee in a pan and add the grated carrots. Saut√© until the carrots are cooked and the raw smell disappears. Pour in the milk and cook the carrots on low heat until the milk evaporates, stirring occasionally. Add sugar and continue to cook until the mixture thickens. Once the halwa reaches a desired consistency, add chopped nuts, raisins, and cardamom powder. Mix well. Cook for a few more minutes until the nuts are lightly roasted and the halwa reaches a rich, golden color. Remove from heat and serve warm. Garnish with additional nuts if desired.

Enjoy your delicious Gajar Ka Halwa!

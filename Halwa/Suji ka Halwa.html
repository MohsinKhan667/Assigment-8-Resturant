Suji Ka Halwa Recipe

Ingredients:

1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
10-12 raisins 10-12 cashews, chopped 1 cup sugar
2 cups water 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder 1 cup semolina (suji)

Instructions:

Heat ghee in a pan, add semolina, and roast it on low flame until it turns golden brown and gives a nutty aroma. Meanwhile, in a separate saucepan, heat water and sugar to make a sugar syrup. Stir until the sugar dissolves. Slowly add the sugar syrup to the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Keep stirring until the semolina absorbs the water and the mixture thickens. Add cardamom powder and continue to cook until the halwa reaches the desired consistency. In a small pan, fry cashews until they are golden brown. Add raisins and fry for a few more seconds until they plump up. Pour the fried cashews and raisins over the halwa and mix well. Remove from heat and serve warm.

Enjoy your delicious Suji Ka Halwa!

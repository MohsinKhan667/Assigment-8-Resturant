Mutton Karahi Recipe

Ingredients:

    1 kg mutton, cut into pieces, 2 cups yogurt, 3 tomatoes, chopped, 1 large onion, finely sliced, 3 green chilies, chopped, 3 cloves garlic, minced, 1-inch ginger, grated, 1/2 cup cooking oil, 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala, Fresh coriander leaves for garnis, Salt to tast.

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a karahi or deep pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

2. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.

3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.

4. Add mutton pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.

5. Stir in chopped tomatoes, green chilies, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.

6. Pour in yogurt and mix well. Cover and simmer until the mutton is tender.

7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serve hot karahi with naan.

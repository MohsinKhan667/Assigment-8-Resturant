Chicken Karahi Recipe

Ingredients:

    1 kg chicken, cut into pieces 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 large onion, thinly sliced 2 tomatoes, chopped 2 green chilies, chopped 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon garam masala Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

    Heat oil in a karahi or wok over medium heat.
    Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
    Stir in ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
    Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
    Add tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
    Cook until tomatoes are soft.
    Cover and simmer until the chicken is fully cooked.
    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    Serve hot with naan or rice.

Enjoy your delicious chicken karahi!

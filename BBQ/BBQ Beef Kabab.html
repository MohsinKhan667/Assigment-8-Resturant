BBQ Beef Kabab Recipe

Ingredients:

    1 pound beef, thinly sliced, 1/4 cup soy sauce,
    2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 cloves garlic, minced,
    1 teaspoon ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon paprika,
    1 teaspoon cumin, Wooden skewers, soaked in water,

Instructions:

    In a bowl, mix soy sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, black pepper, paprika, and cumin. Add thinly sliced beef to the marinade, making sure it's well-coated. Marinate for at least 30 minutes. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread marinated beef slices onto soaked wooden skewers. Grill the beef kababs for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked to your liking. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious BBQ Beef Kababs!

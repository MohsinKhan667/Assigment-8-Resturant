BBQ Chicken Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:

    2 lbs boneless chicken pieces
    1 cup plain yogurt
    2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
    1 tablespoon red chili powder
    1 tablespoon garam masala
    1 tablespoon lemon juice
    Salt to taste
    Skewers

Instructions:

    In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to form the marinade. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and coat them with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat. Thread marinated chicken pieces onto skewers. Grill the chicken skewers for about 15-20 minutes or until fully cooked, turning occasionally for even cooking. Serve hot with mint chutney and naan.

Enjoy your delicious BBQ Chicken Tikka!

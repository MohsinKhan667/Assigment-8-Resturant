Strawberry Shake Recipe

Ingredients:

2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
2 cups vanilla ice cream 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
1 cup milk Whipped cream for garnish Fresh strawberries for garnish

Instructions:

In a blender, combine fresh strawberries, vanilla ice cream, milk, and sugar. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust sugar if needed. Pour the strawberry shake into glasses. Garnish with a dollop of whipped cream and fresh strawberries. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tip: You can add a handful of ice cubes before blending for a colder and thicker shake.

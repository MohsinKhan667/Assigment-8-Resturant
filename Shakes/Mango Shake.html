Mango Shake Recipe

Ingredients:

2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste) 2 cups cold milk 1 cup ice cubes
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional) Whipped cream and mango slices for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

In a blender, combine diced mangoes, cold milk, sugar, ice cubes, and vanilla extract (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust sugar if needed. Pour the mango shake into glasses. Garnish with whipped cream and mango slices if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Mango Shake!

Tip: You can also add a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an extra indulgent treat.
